How We Work

Pet Adoption Process

Find Your Pet

Consider factors such as the pet's breed, size, energy level, and personality to find the best match for your lifestyle

You can also browse through our to view available pets and learn more about them.

Visit our kennel to meet the pets in person and get to know them

Know Your Pet

Ask staffs at our kennel questions about the pet's background, behavior, and health.

Spend some time with the pet to get a sense of their personality and determine if they are a good fit for your home.

Make sure to ask questions and address any concerns you may have

Take Your Pet Home

Complete the adoption process, which involves filling out an application, paying an adoption fee, and signing an adoption contract.

Make sure to have a comfortable and safe space prepared for your new pet.

The staffs at our kennel can provide information and support as you welcome your new pet into your home.
